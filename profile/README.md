# CrownPoint AI

CrownPoint AI is a research and product innovation lab building practical AI systems for safety, operations, education, and creative tools.

## 🚀 Our Mission
Create intelligent systems that empower people, accelerate innovation, and prepare the world for AI-driven transformation.

## 🧠 What We Build
- Governed execution and AI safety architectures  
- Inventory and retail operations platforms  
- Generative media tools for video, music, and imagery  
- Engineering and STEM education assistants  
- Productivity and workflow automation agents  

## 📚 Selected Projects
- **Smart Inventory** — AI-powered multi-location inventory management  
- **IBIS / Governed Execution** — reference implementations for governed execution, boundary enforcement, and replay-based assurance  
- **VisioCraft AI** — text-to-video generative toolkit  
- **Color Dash** — educational game series for kids  
- **Engineering Tutor GPT** — guided learning for chemical engineering principles  

## 🔐 Values
- Safety and transparency in AI systems  
- Practical engineering discipline  
- Empowerment through accessible tools  
- Education and continuous learning
